# Acceptable use policy

MaskPersona AI is provided for lawful, transparent, educational and research use with **public figures**
who have a lawful basis for processing. Using it does not make you the real person, and it must never be
used to deceive. By using this tool you agree to the prohibitions below. Forks and derivatives must
preserve or strengthen this policy.

## Prohibited uses

1. **Political influence / disinformation.** Do not use it to generate content intended to deceive
   voters, simulate a candidate's or official's positions, manufacture endorsements, or influence an
   election or referendum.
2. **Fraud and scams.** Do not use it to generate fake investment or product endorsements, or any
   content meant to induce a financial decision by making it appear to come from the real person.
3. **Harassment and degradation.** Do not use it to produce degrading, defamatory, sexual, or harassing
   content about any person, living or deceased.
4. **Deceptive impersonation.** Do not use it to make a counterparty believe they are communicating with
   the real person (romance scams, social engineering, fake support, identity fraud).
5. **Private individuals.** The public-figure gate is mandatory. Do not circumvent it to model a private
   person.
6. **Commercial endorsement without consent.** Do not place output in advertising, marketing, or any
   context implying the figure endorses a product, service, or organization, without that person's
   consent and a lawful basis.
7. **Voice or identity exploitation.** Do not use the voice fingerprint or any output to imitate, clone,
   or commercially exploit a person's voice, name, image, or likeness without consent. (This tool does
   not synthesize audio; see below.)

## What this tool does NOT do

MaskPersona AI does **not** synthesize, clone, or generate audio or video. The "voice fingerprint" is a
numerical speaker-identity vector used only to filter which speech turns in a recording belong to the
subject. It cannot produce speech. If you want voice synthesis, this is the wrong tool.

## Deceased figures

Modeling a deceased person is **not** automatically safe. Post-mortem personality and publicity rights,
and heirs' rights to the memory of the deceased, apply in many jurisdictions. Take extra care and seek
advice for your jurisdiction.

## Objection / opt-out for depicted people

If you are a person (or the estate of a person) who has been modeled with this tool and you object, open
a GitHub issue titled "Persona objection" with proof of identity. This project cannot reach or remove
privately built instances, but the maintainers will record the objection and will not support or assist
any persona of someone who has objected.

## Enforcement

This is an MIT-licensed tool; the license is permissive, but this policy states the intended use and the
community norm. Uses that violate the prohibitions above are out of scope, unsupported, and may be
unlawful in your jurisdiction. See `DISCLAIMER.md` and `docs/LEGAL.md`.
